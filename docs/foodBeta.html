Water

Wellness
Amount of wellness gained.
Harvested?
Plants, animal products, minerals.
Green Thumb Level

Wellness Efficiency
Measure of how much wellness is gained by unit of food or water. The smaller the value, the more you gain wellness before being "full". Only computed for positive wellness.
Buffs

Options are All, Player, Dev and None.
